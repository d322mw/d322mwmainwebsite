Acid is an anthropomorphic hybrid Feline Trijet Airplane based on a McDonnell Douglas MD-11 featuring his third engine on his tail. He also can have the same properties as an original feline mixed in with his navigation systems to help him live freely and fly happily out in the wilderness! Sadly, his species is slowly decreasing as newer Avi species take over the planet. Thankfully Acid legancy will live on and carry the losses of his family and his breed.

Features> Has Three engines (Pratt & Whitney PW4000) Along with a stretched and modified version of the DC-10, featuring Day and night glass cockpit windows where during the day Acids sclera is visible. At dusk, his sclera are invisible and only his iris and pupil glow as a way to see during night, winglets help to reduce, and a larger fuselage.
