I'm proud to announce my second version of Acid. His legs are the main landing gear along towards the bottom are his paws! His bottom paw pads are Rubber tires!
I adore his outfit and his small Roland 303 in the little bags which if you didn't know. Acid's name comes from the Techno genre Aka (Acid Techno) Which started between the late '80s and early '90s. Yellow White and Gray are my favorite colors, especially with the black nose and the anti-sun glare just makes planes cute as hell.
For those who also wanna know about the check mark on his tail and in between the cockpit window and the passenger window is from the Commodore Amiga 1200 Computer system which has inspired me to go into PCs, and learn a1out the history even if I wasn't there during the age.
